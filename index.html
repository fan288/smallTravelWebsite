Japan

Korea

Built in 1971, N Seoul Tower is South Korea's first general radio wave tower, providing TV and radio broadcasting in Seoul.

British

The British Museum is a public museum dedicated to human history, art and culture located in the Bloomsbury area of London.

Egypt

The Great Pyramid of Giza - served as the tomb of pharaoh Khufu, who ruled during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom.

Ultimately, the journey not only expands our knowledge but also cultivates a sense of adventure and curiosity that lasts a lifetime.
